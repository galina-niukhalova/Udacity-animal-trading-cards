Tasmanian devil

Tasmanian devil has one of the strongest bites in the animal world. It can bite through the metal trap.

  • Scientific Name: Sarcophilus harrisii
  • Average Length: 61 cm
  • Average Mass: 7 kg
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 5 years

Wild Tasmanian devils can only be found in the wild in Tasmania (Australia). Hundreds of years ago, Tasmanian devils not only lived in Tasmania, but also on the Australian mainland.
